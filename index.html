Dadpe Pohe

Dadpe Pohe is a quick fix snacky dish which is delicious, light on the stomach and is quick to prepare.

Ingredients

  • • Patal Pohe or Kanda Pohe
  • • Finely chopped onions
  • • Finely chopped onions
  • • Chopped coriander
  • • Grated fresh coconut
  • • Mustard seeds
  • • Asoefetida
  • • Ground wet Coconut
  • • Green Chillies
  • • Salt

Preparation

  • • Stir fry the Pohe on slow fire.
  • • Soak Pohe and drain the water completely.
  • • Keep it for an hour or two.
  • • Mix it with finely chopped onions, cucumber, chillies, coriander and salt to taste
  • • Heat very little oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, asoefetida and if you want you can add Turmeric
  • • Add to the mixture and mix it well again.

You have a wonderful snack that can be further garnished with Shev or other savouries as you wish.

Methi or Fenugreek Seeds Salad

Methi or Fenugreek is an integral part of Indian cuisine. Often touted as a natural remedy for reduction of cholesterol or even blood sugar levels,

India is a major producer, with the state of Rajasthan accounting for over 80% of India's produce. Fenugreek is used as a herb (dried or fresh leaves), spice (seeds), and vegetable (fresh leaves, sprouts, and microgreens).  Fenugreek can be cooked in its seed form or in its leafy form. In both forms, Fenugreek is bitter.

I became a fan of this vegetable/seed when my mother tried a simple salad whose recipe I am about to share with you.

Ingredients

  • • Dry Methi Salad (To serve 2-3 persons)
  • • Dry Methi: 2 tablespoonful
  • • Onion: 1 small or medium size
  • • Coriander as required
  • • Ground wet Coconut
  • • Green Chillies
  • • Garlic plant leaves chopped finely
  • • Salt

You can add whatever else you feel you would like to have in it. You can try carrots (finely chopped) or Patta Pyaj or Peas (you can crush them a bit after steaming). Go crazy!

Preparation

• Soak the methi seeds overnight.

• Rinse the water and wrap the seeds in a cloth and leave it till it sprouts (could be another day)

• Chop onions finely. Take half of it and fry the onion in a very small quantity of oil till it is translucent.

• Add ground wet coconut, chopped garlic leaves, chopped coriander, fried onions, unfried onions, chopped green chilli and salt to taste. You can add any seasoning like basil, oregano.
